Ping Pong Ball Launcher Project Computer Science Period 6

Backwards Unicarn

At the beginning of Trimester 3, our class's engineering and coding skills came into play for the Ping Pong Launcher. The objective was to launch a ping pong ball over a net with the use of an Arduino.

My partner,

  • Tiana,
    • and I came up with a Unicorn Carriage. This Unicorn Carriage was a lot like a backwards horse carriage because the Unicorn was dragged by the Carrige. Going based off of the Remote-Controlled Robot on the Arduino kit, Tiana and I were able to move the carriage back and forth with a simple command, and the Unicorn would launch forward along with the ping pong ball.
