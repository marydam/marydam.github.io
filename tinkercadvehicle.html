TinkerCad Vehicle Computer Science Period 6

After the TinkerCad House in Trimester 3, our class's design, creativity, and Arduino skills came into play for the TinkerCad House. The purpose was to be able to build our own vehicle after working with the TinkerCad House and incorporate a 3D printed object with Arduino components.

Each vehicle was graded on extra details outside of the provided example. My vehicle was a Pull Wagon that included a cup holder, a space for 4 Arduino wheels, a space for a BreadBoard, and a space for a RedBoard.
